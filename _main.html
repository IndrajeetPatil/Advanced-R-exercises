About

My solutions to exercises from Hadley Wickham’s Advanced R (2nd edition) book.

I’ve tried my best to make sure that I have not included any incorrect solutions by consulting the official solutions manual which is co-authored by the original author. That said, I provide no guarantees that all solutions are correct. Additionally, when I disagreed with the proposed official solutions or found them to be inadequate or incorrect, I’ve stuck with my own solutions.
